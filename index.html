Process

From county record
to closed deal.

Four steps between a lis pendens filing and a check in your hand.

Step 01
Lead Discovery
RPA automation scrapes lis pendens filings from county records twice weekly. New cases appear in your dashboard within hours of being filed.
Step 02
Case Creation
Each lead becomes a fully tracked case with owner data, property address, court timeline, and status classification — all pre-populated automatically.
Step 03
Homeowner Outreach
Send SMS, calls, or emails from the Workspace inbox. AI drafts context-aware follow-ups. Every message is linked to the case for full history.
Step 04
Deal Execution
Use ARV analysis and deal notes to structure your offer. Full case documentation accessible at every stage — from first contact to closing.
